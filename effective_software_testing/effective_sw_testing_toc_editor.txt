                      Effective Software Testing - 2nd edition

                                                    Mauricio Aniche

The document proposes the second edition of Effective Software Testing.
In a nutshell, this new edition will:

• Be more foundational and programming-language agnostic.
• Several improvements based on reader feedback.
• Focus on how AI and agentic AI can help in the process.
